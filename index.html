About Us

Villa Napoli is a luxury Italian restaurant inspired by the culinary traditions of Naples. We combine authentic recipes, premium ingredients, and elegant hospitality to deliver a refined dining experience.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for Italian gastronomy, Villa Napoli began as a vision to bring traditional flavors to a modern setting. Every dish reflects heritage, craftsmanship, and love for Italian cuisine.

Starters / Antipasti

  • Bruschetta – Grilled bread topped with tomatoes, basil, garlic, and olive oil.
  • Caprese Salad – Fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, basil, and balsamic glaze.
  • Garlic Bread – Oven-baked bread with garlic butter, herbs, and parmesan.

Pizza

  • Margherita – Tomato sauce, mozzarella, and fresh basil.
  • Quattro Formaggi – Mozzarella, gorgonzola, parmesan, and fontina.
  • Pepperoni – Pepperoni, mozzarella, and rich tomato base.

Pasta

  • Pasta Alfredo – Creamy parmesan sauce with garlic and butter.
  • Carbonara – Eggs, pecorino cheese, pancetta, and black pepper.
  • Mushroom Risotto – Arborio rice cooked with mushrooms and parmesan.

Desserts

  • Tiramisu – Espresso-soaked ladyfingers with mascarpone cream.
  • Panna Cotta – Smooth vanilla cream with berry topping.
  • Italian Coffee – Authentic espresso brewed from Italian beans.

Beverages & Drinks

  • Red Wine – Premium Italian red wine selection.
  • Cocktails – Handcrafted Italian-inspired cocktails.
  • Soft Drinks – Refreshing carbonated beverages.

Chef’s Specials

  • Truffle Pizza – Gourmet pizza infused with truffle oil.
  • Lobster Ravioli – Handmade ravioli with creamy tomato sauce.
